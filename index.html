My Favourite Hobby
Reading Books

My hobby is reading both, interesting and knowledgeable books. After completing my home work and Whitehat Jr projects I just like to read books. Now I know that reading books have a mountain of advantages, I will list a few- Reading enhances your vocabulary, Boosts your imagination, Makes you enter a world full of knowledge and facts and fantasy. Back to me, I love the Adventurous books of Geronimo Stilton because whenever I read them I make a beautiful world of my own in my mind. And of course I love reading knowledgeable books like Books on History, Science etc. Reading books is a good source of enjoyment, knowledge and inspiration. It makes us disciplned, punctual and most importantly a successful person in life.

HAPPY READING!

My Favourite Book
The Quest for Paradise, The Return to the Kingdom of Fantasy

Although all the books of the Geronimo Stilton Series written by Elisabetta Maria Dami are my Favourite Books, I love "The Quest for Paradise, The Return to the Kingdom of Fantasy" book the most which is also authored by Elisabetta Maria Dami. I like this book the most because in this book Geronimo travels all the Seven Rainbow Kingdoms of Fantasy with his Friends!
